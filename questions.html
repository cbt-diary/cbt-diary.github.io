About this exercise

You can practice your appreciation or acceptence by writing things you are grateful for or things you resist. Being grateful is about noticing good things in your life. Acceptence is about noticing when you resist something. You don't have to do this every day, optimal amount would be once in a few days, depending on your needs.

What/Who do you apreciate the most in your life? Write at least 3 items.

What do you resist? Write at least 3 items (feelings, thoughts, people, emotions, places).
